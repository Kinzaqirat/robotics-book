# Weekly Breakdown

This curriculum is designed to be covered over a 12-week period, with each week focusing on a specific set of topics.

### Week 1: Introduction to Physical AI
- **What is Physical AI?**: Understanding the core concepts and the shift from digital to physical intelligence.
- **History**: A brief overview of the history of humanoid robotics.
- **Key Concepts**: Introduction to fundamental terminology and ideas in the field.

### Week 2: Anatomy of Humanoid Robots
- **Structural Components**: Exploring the mechanical design of humanoid robots.
- **Sensors**: Understanding the different types of sensors used in robotics.
- **Kinematics & Dynamics**: The principles of robot movement and forces.

### Week 3: Perception I - Vision
- **Computer Vision**: How robots see and interpret the world.
- **Deep Learning for Vision**: Applying neural networks to visual data.

### Week 4: Perception II - Auditory and Tactile Sensing
- **Auditory & Speech Processing**: Enabling robots to hear and understand spoken language.
- **Tactile & Force Sensing**: How robots feel and interact with their environment through touch.

### Week 5 & 6: Control Systems
- **Whole-Body Control**: Coordinating the entire robot's body for complex actions.
- **Balance & Locomotion**: The science behind making robots walk and maintain balance.
- **Manipulation & Grasping**: How robots pick up and handle objects.

### Week 7 & 8: Machine Learning in Robotics
- **Reinforcement Learning**: Training robots through trial and error.
- **Imitation Learning**: Teaching robots by demonstration.
- **Deep Learning**: Advanced neural network architectures for robotic tasks.

### Week 9: Human-Robot Interaction (HRI)
- **Safe Interaction**: Ensuring safety in human-robot collaboration.
- **Social Communication**: Designing robots that can interact with people in a natural way.

### Week 10: Ethical Considerations
- **Ethical Challenges**: Discussing the ethical implications of advanced AI and robotics.
- **Frameworks for Responsible AI**: Exploring guidelines for developing ethical AI.

### Week 11: Applications of Physical AI
- **Real-World Applications**: Case studies of humanoid robots in various industries.
- **Emerging Technologies**: A look at the cutting-edge of robotics research.

### Week 12: The Future of Humanoid AI & Final Projects
- **Future of Humanoid AI**: Speculating on the long-term impact of physical AI.
- **Final Project Presentations**: Students present their work and findings.
